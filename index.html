    Calaguas Amazing Race - Island Adventure Beach, Trail, Multi-Mountain Summit Challenge! Jun 09, 10 and 11, 2013

    Calaguas Island is a true gem located in the province of Camarines Norte, in the Bicol Region of the Philippines. This pristine paradise is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush greenery. It has become a popular destination for both local and foreign tourists who are looking for a secluded and tranquil getaway. The island is comprised of two major beaches, namely Mahabang Buhangin and Tinaga Beach. Mahabang Buhangin is the most popular of the two, with its long stretch of powdery white sand, and crystal-clear waters that offer a perfect view of the surrounding islands.
